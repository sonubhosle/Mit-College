About Us

MIT College of Computer Science and IT is situated in Basmath, a prominent town in the Hingoli district of Maharashtra. The college was established with the vision of providing quality education in the field of computer science and information technology to rural and semi-urban students.

It is affiliated with Swami Ramanand Teerth Marathwada University, Nanded. Basmath enjoys good connectivity by road and rail to major cities in Maharashtra, making the college accessible to students from neighboring regions. The town holds cultural and historical significance in the Marathwada region. The MIT College campus is set in a peaceful environment that promotes learning and growth. Surrounded by greenery and thoughtfully designed infrastructure, the campus offers a serene and student-friendly atmosphere that encourages academic excellence and personal development. With a focus on discipline, innovation, and holistic education, MIT College is committed to nurturing skilled professionals and responsible citizens for the future.

Our Vision

To be a leading and inclusive center of excellence in Computer Science and IT, empowering students—especially from rural and semi-urban backgrounds—with cutting-edge knowledge, innovation, and strong ethical values. We strive to nurture socially responsible, technically skilled professionals ready to drive positive change in a global, technology-driven world.
